Our Story

Crafting Beauty,
Creating Confidence

Founded with a passion for beauty and elegance, Glam Makeover Studio is Pondicherry's premier luxury unisex salon located on Mettupalaiyam 80ft Road. What started as a boutique studio with a simple vision — to make everyone feel extraordinary — has blossomed into a full-service luxury salon trusted by over 1,500 happy clients.

Our team of certified professionals brings expertise to every appointment, ensuring you leave feeling your absolute best.

🎯 Our Mission

To deliver transformative beauty experiences that empower every client to step out feeling their absolute best.

🔭 Our Vision

To be Pondicherry's most trusted luxury salon, setting the standard for innovation and inclusive beauty.
